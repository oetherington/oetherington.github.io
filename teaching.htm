Teaching

Click to book a lesson!

I also offer lessons on piano, electric bass and double bass for children and adults of any skill level from beginners up to and including professionals, although I specialise in teaching intermediate/advanced musicians. I primarily focus on jazz, blues and soul but can teach whatever style of music the student prefers.

In lessons, I cover technique, theory, sight reading, playing by ear, transcription, notation, repertoire, harmony, improvisation, playing in a group and any other skills that are desired with a lesson plan carefully tailored to each student.

I can travel to students' homes within London zones 1-6, as well as parts of Surrey, Kent, Essex and the surrounding areas. For students located further away, I also offer lessons via Skype or Google Hangouts.

Lessons may be booked one at a time or, alternatively, several weeks may be booked in advance for a reduced price.

I can also prepare students for ABRSM or LCM jazz exams.
