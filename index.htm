Ollie Etherington
London based pianist and bassist



Ollie Etherington is a London based pianist, electric bassist and double bassist available for performing, recording and lessons. He specializes in soul, jazz, blues, stride, boogie woogie and dixieland music, but can play or teach whatever style is desired.

Ollie is currently primarily working with Georgia & The Vintage Youth, regularly performing and recording, as well as doing occasional radio and TV appearances. Previously, he studied music at The BRIT School, piano with Tim Richards, Malcolm Edmonstone and Julie Parker and double bass with Derek Baxter.

He also spent several years as the pianist and leader of a 15 piece big band, and worked as musical director with the The Very Clever Theatre Company on the first ever amateur production of "Avenue Q" for which he won the 2009 KAF award for "Best Direction of Music". He's also played in a number of other productions including 'Godspell', 'The Lion King' and 'Oliver'.

Teaching

Click to book a lesson!

I also offer piano, electric bass, double bass and music theory lessons for children and adults of any skill level from beginners up to and including professionals, although I specialise in teaching intermediate/advanced musicians. I primarily focus on jazz, blues and soul but can teach whatever style of music the student prefers. I can also prepare students for ABRSM, Trinity Guildhall or LCM exams with either classical or jazz syllabuses.

I cover technique, theory, sight reading, playing by ear, transcription, notation, repertoire, harmony, improvisation, playing in a group and any other skills that are desired with a lesson plan carefully tailored to each student.

I can travel to students' homes within 15 miles of Croydon and for students located further away or with difficult schedules, I also offer online lessons via Skype or Google Hangouts at a reduced price.

Lessons may be booked one at a time or, alternatively, several weeks may be booked in advance for a block discount.

I also hold an enhanced DBS check for teaching children.
